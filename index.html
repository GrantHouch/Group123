What is SmartSchedule?

SmartSchedule is an all-in-one calendar app designed to streamline your academic life. Seamlessly integrating with your Brightspace calendar and consolidating all your classes and schedules into a single, user-friendly interface, it offers unparalleled convenience. From managing coursework deadlines to organizing class timings, SmartSchedule simplifies your academic routine by providing a centralized platform for comprehensive schedule management.
